World's best Lasagna

Description

This recipe is quick, easy and customizable. The full recipe is listed below but you can make substitutions if you like.

Ingredients

  • Meat: A blend of Italian Sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onoin and Garlic: A whole onion and two cloves of garlic are recommended.
  • Tomatos: You'll need a mix of crushed tomoatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomatoe paste.
  • Sugar:Two table spoons are recommended.
  • Spices and seasonings: You'll need fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Store bought or homemade are fine
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzerella, and ricotta are recommended.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so that it does not ooze out of the dish.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
